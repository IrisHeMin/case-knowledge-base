### 1. 概述 (Overview)

这份文档是为**经济学零基础学习者**设计的完整学习路线图。它将经济学的庞大知识体系整理成一棵**由浅入深的知识树**，帮助你从「完全不懂经济学」到「能理解经济现象、分析经济政策、形成独立思考」。

经济学看似高深复杂，充满了模型和公式，但其实它的核心非常贴近日常生活。**经济学就是研究人如何做选择的学科**——当资源有限时，我们如何分配？企业如何定价？政府为什么要干预市场？这些问题你每天都在经历。

这份路线图将经济学知识分为 **7 个层级**，前 4 层聚焦**微观经济学**（个体行为），后 3 层聚焦**宏观经济学**（整体经济），每个层级都包含核心知识点、经典案例和推荐学习资源。

### 3. 学习路径建议

| 阶段 | 层级 | 学习目标 | 建议时间 |
|------|------|---------|---------|
| **入门** | Level 1 | 理解经济学的基本思维方式 | 1 周 |
| **微观基础** | Level 2-3 | 掌握供需分析和决策理论 | 2-3 周 |

> 💡 **建议学习方式**：每学完一个层级，尝试用学到的知识分析一条财经新闻。经济学最好的学习方式就是**与现实对照**。

### 1. Overview

This document is a complete learning roadmap designed for **economics beginners**. It organizes the vast field of economics into a **progressive knowledge tree**, helping you go from "knowing nothing about economics" to "understanding economic phenomena, analyzing economic policies, and forming independent thinking."

Economics may seem complex, full of models and formulas, but at its core, it's deeply connected to everyday life. **Economics is the study of how people make choices** — when resources are limited, how do we allocate them? How do businesses set prices? Why does the government intervene in markets? These are questions you encounter every day.

This roadmap divides economics into **7 levels**: the first 4 focus on **Microeconomics** (individual behavior), and the last 3 focus on **Macroeconomics** (the economy as a whole). Each level includes core knowledge points, classic case studies, and recommended learning resources.

### 3. Suggested Learning Path

| Phase | Level | Learning Goal | Suggested Time |
|-------|-------|--------------|----------------|
| **Getting Started** | Level 1 | Understand basic economic thinking | 1 week |
| **Micro Basics** | Level 2-3 | Master supply-demand analysis & decision theory | 2-3 weeks |
| **Micro Advanced** | Level 4 | Understand market structures & failures | 1-2 weeks |
| **Macro Basics** | Level 5 | Master macroeconomic indicators | 1-2 weeks |
| **Macro Advanced** | Level 6-7 | Understand economic policy & international economics | 2-3 weeks |

> 💡 **Tip**: After completing each level, try analyzing a financial news story using what you've learned. The best way to learn economics is to **connect it with reality**.
